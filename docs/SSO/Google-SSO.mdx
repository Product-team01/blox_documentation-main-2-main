## Navigate to the APIs & Services section within your Google Cloud Service.

![Step 1](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/image-20240527-142050.png)
**Select Credentials**

![Step 2](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/image-20240422-100644.png)
**Create an OAuth Client ID credentials and choose Application type as Web application.**

![Step 3](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/image-20240422-100901.png)

**Copy the client ID, client secret from the created Application**

![Step 4](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/image-20240527-142217.png)
**Paste the Client ID, secret key into Vue’s SSO configuration screen and click on Confirm**

![Step 5](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/image-20240527-143413.png)
**Copy & Paste the generated Redirect URI’s to Authorized redirect URI's in your application**

![Step 6](https://d1r1e7xjkfj7nz.cloudfront.net/image-20240527-142814.png)
